# Source Document Mapping

This documents which template source files were used to build each section of the website, and where each PDF is available for download.

## Source Files (templateDocs/)

| Source File | Content | Used For | Public PDF |
|-------------|---------|----------|------------|
| `Handbook.docx` | Full resident handbook with rules, schedule, expectations | House rules on About page, FAQ answers, program-info.ts data | `Isaac_Recovery_Home_Handbook.pdf` |
| `Pre.docx` | Mission statement and orientation materials | Home page mission statement, About page program overview | `Isaac_Recovery_Home_Orientation.pdf` |
| `Document (6).docx` | Employment and Accountability Policy | Employment rules in program-info.ts, About page daily schedule | `Isaac_Recovery_Home_Employment_Policy.pdf` |

| `collage.png` | Logo with cross, dove, and Philippians 4:6-7 | Navigation logo, home page hero image | `images/logo.png` |

## Content Mapping by Page

### Home Page (`/`)
- **Mission statement**: Derived from `Pre.docx` orientation materials
- **Program highlights**: Summarized from `Handbook.docx` and `Document (6).docx`
- **Scripture quotes**: Philippians 4:6-7 (from `collage.png` branding), Mark 12:31 (from `Handbook.docx`)
- **"How it works" steps**: Based on intake process from `Full_Packet.pdf`

### About Page (`/about`)
- **Program overview**: From `Pre.docx` and `Handbook.docx`
- **House rules**: Directly from `Handbook.docx` rules sections
- **Daily schedule**: From `Document (6).docx` employment requirements + `Handbook.docx` curfew/meetings
- **Director info**: From `Full_Packet.pdf` (Justin Gomez Medrano, 940-232-8252)
- **Downloads**: All 6 PDFs available

### Admissions Page (`/admissions`)
- **Intake form fields**: Mirror `Full_Packet.pdf` exactly:
  - Step 1 (Personal): Name, phone, email, emergency contact, move-in date
  - Step 2 (Substance): Drug of choice, length of use, last use date
  - Step 3 (Medical): Conditions, medications, mental health, allergies
  - Step 4 (Legal): Criminal background, probation/parole, pending cases
  - Step 5 (Statement): Why join, why sobriety
  - Step 6 (Agreement): House rules from `Handbook.docx`

### FAQ (`/about`)
- **Eligibility**: From `Pre.docx` (adult men 18+)
- **Cost**: From `Handbook.docx` ($150/week)
- **Employment**: From `Document (6).docx` (daily search 9-4, 3-4 apps)
- **Faith program**: From `Pre.docx` and `Handbook.docx`
- **Drug testing**: From `Handbook.docx` (zero tolerance, random testing)
- **Curfew**: From `Handbook.docx` (10 PM weekday, 11 PM weekend)
- **Visitors**: From `Handbook.docx` (no unauthorized guests)

### Resources Page (`/resources`)
- **Not from template docs** - These are external recovery resources assembled for the site
- Crisis hotlines (988, SAMHSA), AA/NA meeting finders, employment resources, family support, faith resources

### Privacy & Terms
- **Liability section**: Based on `Document (7).docx` personal responsibility policy
- **Service description**: Based on all template documents collectively
